  • Do you provide a guarantee?

    Yes, we provide a 10-year warranty on the property we built. It splits into two periods. We fix every issue with fixtures and fittings that arise during the operation for three years (except for problems caused by the client). A 10-years warranty covers each defect of load-bearing elements (foundations, stairs, ceilings, walls, and roofs), waterproofing, plumbing, and electricals getting damaged. You can find complete information about all that in the documents.

  • I can buy land and build a villa without any help. That would be cheaper?

    First, foreigners cannot buy land in Bali. It is possible to get a land lease for 25 years. The area should not be agricultural or “green”. Second, you need to get initial permissions and a license for building a villa. Third, this process requires a detailed plan and a large group of professionals. It is necessary to take into account many technical moments regarding the legal and building aspects. There is a strong chance you’ll be wasting your money and time if you have no experience in this area. We are familiar with many instances when people could not finish the building process due to issues that have to do with the processes we described.
